Project Overview

CREATING A STORY

Imagine our lives as bookshelves, piled high with personality, and possibility. Broadband strengthens our stories with a world of knowledge. But what about those whose lives are disconnected? Picture the future, one you have touched hands with, but others have not. In collaboration with local stakeholders, we are actively shaping a regional Connectivity Improvement Plan.
